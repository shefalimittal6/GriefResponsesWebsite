About Us


This study is a research endeavor and a part of BTP Project focusing on the impact of
grief-induced sleep disturbances among college students. Through an anonymous
platform, students are encouraged to share their experiences with sleeplessness
stemming from various sources of grief, ranging from academic pressures to personal
losses. The project aims to understand the diverse manifestations of grief within the
college population. Additionally, a contact number is provided for students who feel
comfortable engaging in direct conversations with the researcher, facilitating a deeper
exploration of their challenges and coping mechanisms.
